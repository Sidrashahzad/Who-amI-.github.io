Cultural celebrations

Ramadan

Ramadan is a religious celebration which is celebrated all around
the world by muslims.It is a whole month of fasting, all muslims fast
from sunrise to sunset. They are not allowed to
eat or drink at this time.

Eid ul Fitr

Eid ul fitr marks the end of Ramadan,It is celebrated in many
different ways in different countries.Generally people celebrate
it by giving money/gifts and eating food.

Eid ul Adha

On the day of Eid ul Adha muslims take part in sacraficing an animal
(mainly a sheep,goat,cow,).One third of the meat is to be eaten by the
family and the rest is given to poor.
